About Me

I'm passionate about building technology that feels like a natural extension of human capability — end-to-end systems that reduce cognitive friction by actively assisting users in tasks.

Specifically, I use tools from machine learning, algorithms, data science, robotics, and mechatronics to develop technological solutions that help people achieve their goals efficiently. I'm drawn to the gap between where technology works in theory and where it needs to work in the real world for users.

Beyond engineering, I'm fascinated by cognitive science and neuroscience — understanding human intelligence to build more meaningful AI. Outside of research, you'll find me on the tennis court or soccer field, or out hiking, swimming, and biking.
