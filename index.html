About

A bit about me

I'm passionate about and understanding AI that feels like a natural extension of human capability — intelligent systems that reduce cognitive friction by actively assisting in the physical world.

I combine machine learning, robotics, and mechatronics to develop technology that helps people achieve their goals more efficiently. I'm particularly drawn to the gap between where AI works in simulation and where it needs to work in the real world.

Beyond engineering, I'm fascinated by cognitive science and neuroscience — understanding human intelligence to build more meaningful AI. Outside of research, you'll find me on the tennis court or soccer field, or out hiking, swimming, and biking.
